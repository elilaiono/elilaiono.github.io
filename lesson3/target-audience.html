Target Audience

This website is made for all the people who live in the area of the forecast. It's also meant for anyone who's curious about upcoming forecasts. It's meant for people that are normally out and about and rely on good weather patterns.

Persona #1:

Joe Mamaduke

Joe is an average working man. He's married with three kids and one on the way. On the weekends he loves to take his family hiking in the mountains.

  • "What's the weather going to be like this upcoming weekend?"
  • "Is it necessary that I bring a jacket?"
  • "If it gets below 32 degrees, then I wont bring my youngest child.

Persona #2:

Karen Barrit

Karen is a mother of 4 who is constantly traveling for work. She enjoys being in the car by herself listening to music. She drives around four hours everyday.

  • "I heard there was a storm coming in a few days, it that true?"
  • "Do I need to stay off the roads today?
